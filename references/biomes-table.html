Tropical Rainforest Amazon, Congo, SE Asia Hot, Humid 2000–10000 mm 20–34°C Dense, layered canopy Primates, Birds, Insects Oxisols Broad leaves, drip tips 🌞☔🔄🌱
Constant warmth & rainfall → high biodiversity; stratified canopy maximizes light capture. Rapid nutrient cycling drives competition.
Flora: broad evergreen leaves, drip tips, epiphytes. Fauna: arboreal primates, camouflaged insects. Shallow soil adaptations.
Savanna / Grassland Sub-Saharan Africa, Brazil, India Warm, Seasonal 500–1500 mm 20–30°C Grasses with scattered trees Elephants, Antelopes, Lions Alfisols Deep roots, fire-resistant bark 🌞💧🔥🐐🐘
Fire and grazing maintain open grasslands; seasonal rainfall favors drought-resistant plants; herbivores shape vegetation.
Flora: fire-tolerant trees, perennial grasses. Fauna: migratory herbivores, burrowing rodents.
Desert Sahara, Arabian, Gobi Arid 0–250 mm 20–45°C Sparse shrubs, cacti Camels, Lizards, Scorpions Aridisols Water storage tissues, nocturnal activity 🌞💧🏜️
Extreme heat & aridity drive water conservation; nutrient-poor soils select for slow-growing plants.
Flora: succulent leaves, deep roots. Fauna: nocturnal, burrowing, water storage.
Mediterranean / Chaparral Mediterranean Basin, California, South Australia Hot dry summer, mild wet winter 300–1000 mm 10–30°C Sclerophyll shrubs, evergreen trees Goats, Foxes, Birds Ultisols, Inceptisols Fire-resistant leaves, drought tolerance 🌞💧🔥🐐🔄
Seasonal drought and periodic fires shape shrubland structure and biodiversity.
Flora: thick leaves, resprouting after fire. Fauna: burrowing and nocturnal behavior, diet adaptation.
Temperate Forest Eastern USA, Europe, China Moderate, Seasonal 750–1500 mm 0–25°C Deciduous trees, mixed understory Deer, Foxes, Birds Alfisols Deciduous leaves, hibernation 🔄☔🌱🐐
Seasonal variation drives leaf shedding; fertile soils allow diverse understory growth.
Flora: deciduous trees, layered canopy. Fauna: hibernation, migration, seed dispersal adaptations.
Steppe Central Asia, Eastern Europe Semi-arid, Seasonal 250–500 mm -5–25°C Grasses, sparse shrubs Rodents, Horses, Predators Chestnut soils Drought tolerance, fire resistance 💧🔄🔥🐐
Grass-dominated vegetation maintained by grazing and periodic fires; limited rainfall selects drought-adapted species.
Flora: drought-resistant grasses. Fauna: burrowing rodents, migratory grazers, predator-prey dynamics.
Taiga / Boreal Canada, Russia, Scandinavia Cold, Seasonal 300–850 mm -40–20°C Coniferous trees, mosses Moose, Wolves, Lynx Podzols Needle leaves, antifreeze compounds ❄️🔄🌱
Cold winters limit growing season; coniferous forests adapted to snow and poor soils.
Flora: conifer needles, resin for frost. Fauna: thick fur, hibernation, migration.
Tundra Arctic, Alpine regions Very cold, harsh 150–250 mm -50–10°C Mosses, Lichens, Dwarf shrubs Caribou, Arctic Fox, Lemmings Gelisols Permafrost tolerance, low stature ❄️💧🪨
Extreme cold and short growing seasons drive low biodiversity and specialized growth forms.
Flora: low-growing, cushion plants. Fauna: seasonal migration, insulation, fat storage.
Mangroves / Wetlands SE Asia, Florida, Africa coasts Tropical/Subtropical 1000–4000 mm 20–30°C Salt-tolerant trees, emergent plants Crocodiles, Fish, Birds Hydric, saline soils Aerial roots, salt excretion 🌞💦🌊
Salinity and waterlogging drive aerial roots and salt excretion; wetlands act as nutrient traps and breeding grounds.
Flora: mangroves, salt-tolerant plants. Fauna: tidal-adapted fish, birds; adaptations to fluctuating water levels.
Coral Reef Great Barrier Reef, Caribbean Warm, Tropical 1000–2000 mm 20–30°C Coral polyps, symbiotic algae Fish, Sea Turtles, Invertebrates Calcareous substrate Symbiosis, calcium skeletons 🌞☔🪸🐟
High biodiversity maintained by mutualistic relationships, clear shallow waters, and warm temperatures. Competition for light and space drives complex 3D structures.
Flora: corals, symbiotic algae. Fauna: reef fish, turtles, invertebrates; adaptations for reef niches and grazing pressure.
